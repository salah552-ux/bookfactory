## CURRENT AMAZON REVIEW GUIDELINES (sourced via WebSearch 2026-05-14)

**What authors are permitted to do:**
- Ask readers to leave an honest review in back matter
- Provide a direct link to the Amazon review page
- Send ARC (advance reader copy) books to reviewers without requiring a review in return
- Mention the review request once — not in repeated follow-up campaigns

**What is prohibited:**
- Incentivised reviews — offering anything of value (free products, discounts, payment, gift cards, refunds) in exchange for a review
- Review gating — selectively asking only readers who signal positive experience to leave reviews
- Review swaps — exchanging reviews with other authors
- Requiring a review in exchange for a free ARC copy
- Soliciting only positive reviews — the ask must be for honest experience
- Excessive email campaigns asking for reviews repeatedly post-purchase

**QR codes / links:** Direct links to the Amazon review page are permitted in ebooks. The `[AMAZON_REVIEW_LINK]` placeholder in back matter is compliant once populated with the correct URL.
