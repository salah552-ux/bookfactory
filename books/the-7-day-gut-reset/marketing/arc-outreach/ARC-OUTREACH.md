## Why drafts, not 20 sent emails

The task asked for ARC outreach to "20 targets." Two hard constraints shaped the build:

1. **No real ARC contacts exist.** The Gmail account has zero prior ARC
   correspondence and the pipeline holds no contact list. Inventing 20
   recipient addresses would violate the no-assumptions / data-first rule.
2. **KDP Select exclusivity.** The 7-Day Gut Reset is enrolled in KDP Select
   (PRE-LAUNCH-PLAN Section 1), so ARC copies can ONLY be distributed as Kindle
   gift or direct PDF — never via BookFunnel, NetGalley, StoryOrigin or
   BookSirens (all break Select). That rules out the usual "blast 20 reviewers
   on a platform" motion.

So the deliverable is the *machinery*: paste-ready, personalised-in-one-edit
drafts the Architect sends as real contacts are recruited.
