# About the Author

S.A. Ibrahim writes practical health guides for people who've already done the research and still haven't found what works.

The frustration behind this book is one the author knows from the reader side: the protocols that don't distinguish between different types of gut problems, the elimination diets that remove everything and restore nothing, the generic advice that treats all gut symptoms as the same gut problem. *The 7-Day Gut Reset* was built around a single question none of those books asked first: which type of gut problem is actually yours?

The Fix Your Gut for Good series takes that question further — from the seven-day diagnostic reset into the deeper work of long-term gut repair, microbiome restoration, and the gut-brain mechanisms that make the stress response so difficult to shift without a targeted approach.

*The 7-Day Gut Reset* is the entry-point guide to that series.
