## PRODUCT 1: Day 1 Trigger Log — Printable Tracking Sheet ⭐ PRIORITY

**Type:** Printable
**Format:** PDF (US Letter + A4 — two pages)
**Source content:** Chapter 01-day-one.md — the 5-column trigger log (Time / Food or drink consumed / Stress level 1–10 / Symptom type / Symptom intensity 1–10). The chapter instructs readers to draw this on paper; a pre-formatted log is the natural product extraction.

**Concept:**
A clean, structured 5-column log sheet for tracking gut symptoms across a 24-hour period. Solves the friction of drawing columns by hand. Buyers are gut health readers who want to follow the Day 1 protocol from any gut health book or medical advice — not just this one. The product has standalone value independent of the book.

**Design brief:**
- 2-page PDF. Page 1: the log itself. Page 2: brief instructions + trigger profile identification guide.
- Page 1 layout: header row with column names (Time | Food & Drink | Stress 1–10 | Symptom | Intensity 1–10), then 10 rows — enough for a full day of meals + snacks. Below the log: a "Pattern Notes" box (3 lines).
- Page 2 layout: "Reading Your Log" — three short paragraphs naming the three trigger profiles (food-triggered, stress-triggered, motility-triggered) with 3–4 identification signals each. Title: "What Your Log Is Telling You."
- Colour palette: navy `#1b3a5c` header row + border accents, cream `#f5f2ed` alternating row shading, warm tan `#c8b99a` rule lines.
- Fonts: Playfair Display for page title, Lato for column headers, EB Garamond for body text.
- Both US Letter (8.5×11in) and A4 (210×297mm) versions included.
- Must be print-at-home quality at 300dpi. Design time in Canva: under 2 hours.

**Platform ranking:**
1. Etsy — largest audience for health/wellness printables. Printable journals and trackers are a proven category. Priced at $4.99–$6.99 individual.
2. Gumroad / Payhip — for readers who discover this via the book's back matter CTA. Offer at same price; or free as a lead magnet.
3. Book back matter CTA — a free download offered in the book drives email list sign-ups (use Payhip with email gate).

## PLATFORM LAUNCH SEQUENCE

Recommended order to launch products (maximise momentum):

1. **Etsy — Trigger Log (Product 1)** — First because it's the simplest, cheapest, highest-volume entry point. Establishes the shop in the "gut health tracker" search category before competition for this exact product grows.
2. **Etsy — Personal Maintenance Map (Product 2)** — Within 1 week of Product 1. Together they create an obvious bundle and cross-promote organically via Etsy's "more from this shop" placement.
3. **Etsy — Starter Kit Bundle (Product 4)** — Within 2 weeks of launch. Bundle listing cross-links to all three individual products, driving up average order value.
4. **Etsy — Stress Bridge Practice Card (Product 3)** — Standalone listing benefits from Huberman Lab / vagal tone search traffic independent of the gut health category.
5. **Payhip — Free Trigger Log as lead magnet** — Offered in book back matter CTA page; collects email addresses before the paid Etsy version is well-established.
6. **KDP Print — 30-Day Guided Journal (Product 5)** — After the digital versions are live and generating review data. KDP print requires higher design investment; wait until the market demand from digital versions confirms it.
