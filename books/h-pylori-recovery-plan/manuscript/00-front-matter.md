## How to Use This Book

You can read this book in order, front to back, and it will take you from the moment you tested positive to the day you confirm the infection is gone. That is how it is built — as a path, one step leading to the next.

But you might not be in a reading mood. You might be frightened, or mid-treatment, or staring at a second positive result and wanting one specific answer right now. So here is the shortcut.

If the cancer fear is the thing keeping you awake, go straight to **Chapter 2**. It is the whole reason much of this book exists, and it gives you the real numbers.

If you have just been handed antibiotics and want to know whether they are the right ones, read **Chapter 3**.

If you have already finished a course and you are *still positive*, read **Chapter 5**. That is the chapter almost no other book bothers to write, and it is written for you specifically.

If you just want to know what you are allowed to eat, **Chapter 6**.

If you want to confirm the infection has actually cleared, **Chapter 8**.

And if you want everything distilled onto a single page you can keep, **Chapter 11**.

A note on what this book will and will not do. It will explain the mechanism behind every recommendation, because you deserve to understand *why*, not just be handed instructions. It will cite every statistic to a named, published source — you will find them all in Appendix C. It will never sell you a supplement, a tea, a powder, or a programme. Not once. That is a promise, and it is also the point.

What it will not do is prescribe your treatment. H. pylori is cleared by prescription antibiotics, chosen and supervised by your doctor. This book is the briefing you read *before* that appointment, so you walk in informed and walk out with a plan. Think of it as the friend who happens to have read all the guidelines, sitting across the kitchen table, telling you what they actually say.

Let's begin where you began.
