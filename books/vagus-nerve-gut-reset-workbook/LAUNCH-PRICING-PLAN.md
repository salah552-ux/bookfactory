# LAUNCH PRICING PLAN — The Vagus Nerve Gut Reset Workbook

**Decision owner: THE ARCHITECT** | Pricing strategy approved 2026-06-21 | Master-orchestrator (Opus)

This document defines the launch pricing strategy. The upload agent sets only the base
list prices. The promotional Countdown Deal is a **manual, post-publish action by the
Architect** on the KDP dashboard.

---

## Upload prices (set by kdp-upload-agent at upload time)

| Format | Price (USD) | Notes |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| Kindle (eBook) | **$6.99** | Base list price. This is the value the upload agent enters. |
| Paperback | **$14.99** | Matches both indie leaders (Payne, Bennett @ $14.99). |

- **Primary marketplace: Amazon.com (US) — hard locked.**
- **KDP Select: YES** — enrolled in Kindle Unlimited (required for Countdown Deals).
- **Royalty tier: 70%** ($2.99–$9.99 band covers both the $6.99 base and the $2.99 promo).

---

## Promotional timeline

| Window | Price | Who acts | How |
|--------|-------|----------|-----|
| **Day 1–7** | **$2.99** | Architect (manual) | Schedule a KDP Countdown Deal on the KDP dashboard after the book is live. |
| **Day 8+** | **$6.99** | Automatic | Countdown Deal ends; price auto-reverts to the base list price. No action needed. |

The upload agent does NOT schedule the Countdown Deal. It only sets the $6.99 base price.
A Countdown Deal cannot be created until the book is published and live on Amazon.

---

## Why $2.99 (not $0.99)

- **$2.99 keeps the 70% royalty tier.** Source: KDP royalty rate card — the 70% rate
  applies only to eBooks priced $2.99–$9.99. A $0.99 price drops to the **35% royalty tier**.
- **$0.99 did not buy rank in this niche.** Source: vagus nerve BSR harvest 2026-06-21 —
  bargain-priced ($0.99) titles in the vagus-nerve / nervous-system Kindle tier did not
  achieve rank lift, so the deep discount sacrifices royalty without a ranking payoff.
- $2.99 is a credible launch discount (well below the $6.99 base) that still earns the
  higher royalty AND signals value to early buyers/reviewers.

---

## ⚠️ KDP Countdown Deal eligibility — 30-DAY KDP SELECT RULE (FLAG)

**A KDP Countdown Deal requires the title to be:**
1. Published / live on Amazon.com, AND
2. Enrolled in **KDP Select for at least 30 days** before the deal can run
   (and not have run a Countdown Deal/Free promotion in the immediately prior period).

**FLAG:** If this book was just published, the Day 1–7 Countdown Deal **may not be
eligible immediately** and could need to wait until the 30-day KDP Select enrollment
threshold is met. The Architect must verify Countdown Deal eligibility on the KDP
dashboard at publish time.

**Fallback if the 30-day rule blocks Day 1:**
- Run the $2.99 Countdown Deal as soon as the title becomes eligible (≈Day 30+), OR
- Set the Kindle list price to $2.99 manually at launch for the first 7 days and raise it
  to $6.99 afterward (a manual price change is NOT a Countdown Deal and has no 30-day
  requirement, but loses the Countdown Deal's strike-through/"deal" badging).

The Architect chooses the fallback at publish time based on actual KDP eligibility.
