## Appendix C — Frequently Asked Questions

**How long until I notice something?**
Many people feel a difference in sleep and stress within the first week, and in the gut by Week 3. But trends matter more than days — judge it across weeks, not mornings.

**Do I have to do it at the same time every day?**
No, but a fixed time and an anchor habit make it far easier to keep. Pick the time you're least likely to skip.

**Can I do more than five minutes?**
Yes, if you enjoy it. But more isn't the goal — *consistent* is. Five minutes you do every day beats twenty you do twice.

**I have a heart condition. Is this safe?**
The breathing, sound, and gentle movement exercises are gentle and widely used, but check with your doctor first if you have any medical condition. Skip the cold exercises unless your doctor clears them.

**Is this a replacement for my IBS treatment or medication?**
No. This sits alongside your medical care. Keep taking what you're prescribed and keep your doctor in the loop.

**Do I need a wearable or any device?**
No. The whole program works with a pen and five minutes. HRV tracking is optional and only ever a rough trend.
