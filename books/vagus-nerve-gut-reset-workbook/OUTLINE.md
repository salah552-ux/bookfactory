## Chapter-by-chapter beats

### How to Use This Workbook (00-how-to-use.md)
Set expectations: 5–10 min/day, 28 days, in order. Shortcut paths (jump to Week 3 if gut is your main issue). What it will/won't do (won't diagnose; will cite). The promise: no supplements sold.

### Ch 1 — The 90-Second Vagus Explainer (02)
Hook: the wire most gut books miss. Vagus = cranial nerve X, longest, runs brainstem→gut. 80% afferent = your gut talks to your brain more than the reverse. Vagal tone in plain English (a dimmer switch, not on/off). Cholinergic anti-inflammatory pathway as "the brake on inflammation." End: What This Means For You.

### Ch 2 — Your Baseline (03)
Why measure: reviews love before/after. Self-assessment scales (gut symptoms, bloating, sleep, stress, energy) 0–10. Resting heart rate how-to. Optional HRV via wearable (trend not diagnosis). Fill-in baseline table. End: you now have a number to beat.

### Ch 3 — The Exercise Library (04)
All 12 named exercises, each in the locked EXERCISE TEMPLATE (below). This is the reference readers return to; daily spreads point back here.

### Weeks 1–4 (05–08)
Each week: 1-page theme intro (why this week), then 7 dated daily spreads using the DAILY SPREAD TEMPLATE, then a WEEKLY TRACKER table + weekly reflection.
- Week 1 Foundations: breath-first. Days build sigh → resonance → extended exhale → hum → cold face → neck release → integrate.
- Week 2 Sleep/evening: wind-down stacks, extended exhale at night, OM, voo breath, dark-cold finish, jaw release, evening routine lock-in.
- Week 3 Gut Reset (CORE differentiator): gut-brain axis day, post-meal vagal routine for bloating, the "rest-and-digest" before-eating reset, IBS flare calmer, belly breathing for motility, gut-symptom mapping, stack for digestion.
- Week 4 Stress resilience + automaticity: orienting/safe-connection cue, hum-on-the-go, cold reset under stress, building the personal 10-min stack, troubleshooting, the maintenance plan preview, graduation day.

### Ch 4 — Make It Stick (09)
Build your personal 10-minute stack (menu format). Plateau troubleshooting. Flare-day emergency protocol. Weekly maintenance schedule after the 28 days. When to see a doctor.

### Appendices (14) + Back matter (99)
30-Day Recap table (before/after). Quick-reference exercise index. FAQ. QR companion audio CTA + reader-magnet. Sources & Further Reading (numbered, real). About the Author. Also in the Fix Your Gut for Good series.
