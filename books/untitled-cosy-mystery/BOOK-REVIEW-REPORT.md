## B-CHAPTER FIXES REQUIRED

**Ch 31 (B):** One abstract noun cluster → replace with concrete physical detail. Terminal hook is passive — sharpen final line. Low priority.

**Ch 32 (B) — HIGH PRIORITY:** Opens and runs in first person ("I note that No. 4 has a blue front door"). Only first-person passage in the manuscript. Will be caught by any copy-editor. Convert to third-person close throughout. The observational distance is preserved; only the pronoun register changes.

**Ch 35 (B):** Same first-person slip as Ch 32. Also one summary passage (close reorganisation) — add one concrete observed detail. Convert to third-person close.

**Ch 38 (B):** No forward momentum toward Ch 39–40. Add one gesture in the final lines. Low priority — B is acceptable for a wind-down chapter.
